About

I was raised between Queens NY and San Juan, Puerto Rico, and have called Austin home since 2005.

I started my fitness instructor journey at the age of 17 teaching in an all women's gym in Carolina PR. I used exercise as a way to calm my anxiety, stay focused, and feel good. Some of the classes I have taught throughout the years are water aerobics, cycling, kickboxing, latin dance, barre, and yoga with weights, although my focus for the last 14 years has been mainly yoga. Through extensive training and practice I fell in love with the practice, and my life was changed forever. I am continually exploring meditation and psychology as well as studying anatomy and Asana. I am forever a student of movement! I owned and operated a yoga studio “yoga con amor” for 4 years as I wanted to share the practice with people that were curious about yoga, but might feel intimidated walking into a studio. It was truly YOGA FOR EVERYONE. I love helping my students breathe and calm the chatter of the mind, all while getting strong and healthy.

I am also a mother of two young men, an actress, artist and jewelry maker and business woman.
